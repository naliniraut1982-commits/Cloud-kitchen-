Why we don't cook every day

Sheera is at its best in the first hour off the stove — ghee still glossy, sooji or besan still holding heat, cardamom still sharp. Cooking daily means holding batches, and holding batches means it's never quite fresh.

So we picked two days, cook everything to order, and deliver within hours of the last stir. The rest of the week, the stove is off and we're prepping for the next batch.
